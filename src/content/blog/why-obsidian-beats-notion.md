# Why Obsidian Beats Notion for Personal Knowledge Management

Both tools are excellent, but they serve different use cases.

## Privacy First

Obsidian stores everything locally. Your notes never leave your device unless you choose to sync them.

## Graph View

The interconnected graph view in Obsidian reveals connections between ideas that would remain hidden in a traditional hierarchy.

## Markdown Native

Obsidian uses plain Markdown files. This means your data is portable and future-proof.
