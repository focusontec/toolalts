## Overview

**Ephe** is a minimal, ephemeral markdown editor designed for users who want a lightweight, no-frills writing experience. It focuses on quick note-taking and sharing without permanent storage, making it ideal for temporary or disposable content. As an open-source tool with 200 GitHub stars, Ephe is completely free and runs on a simple, privacy-focused model.

**Airtable** is a powerful low-code platform for building collaborative apps. It combines the flexibility of a spreadsheet with the functionality of a database, enabling teams to create custom workflows, manage projects, and track data at scale. With a 4.6/5 rating from over 2,200 reviews, Airtable is a mature, enterprise-ready solution used by teams of all sizes.

## Pricing

**Ephe** is completely free ($0 USD per user/month). There are no tiers, no hidden costs, and no limits on usage. As an open-source project, you can self-host it or use the public instance at no charge.

**Airtable** offers four pricing tiers:
- **Free**: Unlimited bases, but limited to 1,000 records per base, 1 GB attachment space, and 100 automation runs per month.
- **Team** ($20/seat/month billed annually): 50,000 records per base, 5 GB attachment space, 25,000 automation runs, and extensions.
- **Business** ($45/seat/month billed annually): 500,000 records per base, 20 GB attachment space, 100,000 automation runs, plus SAML SSO, admin panel, and audit logs.
- **Enterprise Scale** (Custom pricing): Everything in Business plus unlimited workspaces, advanced integrations, and dedicated support.

## When to Choose Ephe

Choose Ephe if you need a **fast, disposable note-taking tool** for temporary content. It's perfect for:
- Drafting quick ideas or meeting notes that don't need permanent storage
- Sharing plain markdown snippets with others via a simple link
- Users who value privacy and want an open-source, self-hosted solution
- Minimalists who want zero distractions and no learning curve
- Developers or writers who prefer plain markdown over rich text editors

Ephe excels when you need to capture and share information instantly without worrying about organization, databases, or long-term storage.

## When to Choose Airtable

Choose Airtable if you need a **collaborative app platform** for managing complex data and workflows. It's ideal for:
- Teams tracking projects, inventory, CRM, or content calendars
- Building custom apps without coding (e.g., bug trackers, asset managers)
- Organizations requiring advanced views like Gantt charts or timelines
- Businesses needing enterprise security features (SSO, audit logs, DLP)
- Scaling from small teams to large enterprises with 500,000+ records

Airtable shines when you need structured data, automation, and team collaboration at scale.

## Verdict

Ephe and Airtable serve fundamentally different purposes. **Ephe** is a niche tool for ephemeral markdown notes—it's free, open-source, and intentionally limited. **Airtable** is a full-featured low-code platform for building collaborative apps, with robust pricing and enterprise capabilities.

Choose **Ephe** if you want a lightweight, privacy-focused markdown editor for temporary notes. Choose **Airtable** if you need a scalable, collaborative app builder with advanced features like automation, multiple views, and enterprise security.

For most teams and businesses, Airtable is the clear winner due to its versatility and proven track record. However, if your use case is simply quick, disposable markdown notes, Ephe offers a refreshingly simple alternative at zero cost.
