# Notion vs Obsidian

## Overview

Notion and Obsidian are two of the most popular knowledge management tools, but they take fundamentally different approaches.

## Notion: The All-in-One Workspace

Notion excels at collaboration, databases, and structured content. It's cloud-first and designed for teams.

## Obsidian: The Personal Knowledge Base

Obsidian is built for individuals who want complete control over their data. Its graph view and backlinking system are unmatched.

## Verdict

Choose **Notion** if you work in teams and need databases and real-time collaboration.
Choose **Obsidian** if you prioritize privacy, local storage, and personal knowledge graphs.
