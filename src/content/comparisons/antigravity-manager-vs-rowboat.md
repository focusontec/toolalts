## Overview

**Antigravity-Manager** is a specialized desktop application built with Tauri v2 and React (Rust) that offers one-click seamless account switching for Antigravity Tools. It targets users who manage multiple accounts within the Antigravity ecosystem, providing a fast, secure, and cross-platform experience. With a perfect 4.75/5 rating and a free price tag, it’s a niche tool for a specific workflow.

**Rowboat** is an open-source AI coworker designed to automate tasks and boost productivity through memory retention. It acts as a context-aware assistant that learns from past interactions, making it ideal for developers and knowledge workers who want a customizable, self-hosted AI. Also free and open-source, Rowboat focuses on broader task automation rather than account management.

Both tools are free and open-source, but they serve very different purposes within the productivity category.

## When to Choose Antigravity-Manager

Antigravity-Manager is the clear choice if you:

- **Use Antigravity Tools regularly** – The tool is purpose-built for this ecosystem, offering seamless account switching that no other tool provides.
- **Manage multiple accounts** – If you juggle several Antigravity accounts for work or personal use, the one-click switching saves significant time.
- **Prefer a desktop app** – Built with Tauri v2 and React, it offers a native-like experience with fast performance and low resource usage.
- **Need security** – The application focuses on secure account management, making it suitable for sensitive or professional environments.
- **Want simplicity** – No AI, no complex setup—just install and switch accounts instantly.

## When to Choose Rowboat

Rowboat is the better option if you:

- **Need an AI assistant** – Rowboat’s memory retention allows it to learn from past interactions, providing context-aware help over time.
- **Automate repetitive tasks** – It excels at handling routine work like data entry, scheduling, or code generation.
- **Prefer self-hosting** – As an open-source tool, you can deploy it on your own infrastructure for full control and privacy.
- **Work in a team** – Rowboat is designed to assist individuals and teams, making it suitable for collaborative environments.
- **Value customization** – Community-driven development means you can contribute features or modify the tool to fit your workflow.

## Verdict

Antigravity-Manager and Rowboat are both excellent free tools, but they solve completely different problems. There is no direct competition between them—your choice depends entirely on your needs.

**Choose Antigravity-Manager** if your primary challenge is managing multiple Antigravity Tool accounts. It’s a focused, high-performance desktop app that excels at one specific task: seamless account switching. For anyone in the Antigravity ecosystem, it’s an essential tool.

**Choose Rowboat** if you want an AI coworker that automates tasks and remembers context. It’s a broader productivity tool for developers and knowledge workers who need intelligent assistance across various workflows.

If you use both Antigravity Tools and want AI automation, you could even use them together—Antigravity-Manager for account management and Rowboat for task automation. Since both are free, there’s no reason not to try both.
