## Pricing

### Obsidian
- **Personal**: $0 — Local vault, Community plugins, Graph view
- **Catalyst**: $25 one-time — Early access, Insider builds, Support devs
- **Commercial**: $50/yr — Commercial use, Priority support

### Anki
- **Free**: $0 — All core features, Open source

## When to Choose Obsidian
Consider Obsidian if you need Local-first storage, Graph view, Backlinks.

## When to Choose Anki
Consider Anki if you need AI-generated explanations for flashcards, Automatic mnemonic generation, Illustration generation for visual learning.
