# Slack vs Discord

## Overview

Slack is built for workplace communication. Discord started for gamers but has become a powerful community and team tool.

## Slack

Slack offers polished enterprise features, robust integrations, and a professional interface designed for businesses.

## Discord

Discord provides unlimited message history on free plans, voice channels, and a more casual, community-oriented experience.

## Verdict

Choose **Slack** for professional teams needing enterprise-grade security and integrations.
Choose **Discord** for communities, startups, and teams that value voice channels and free unlimited history.
